01. about me

The story so far

Ahmad is a passionate data engineer with 3 years of professional enterprise experience. He works tirelessly to make sure the data environment is optimized and working as intended. Ahmad holds a Bachelor's in Electrical Power Engineering and two Master's degrees in IT & Computer Science.

While pursuing his Master's degrees, Ahmad started working in IT Help Desk and support roles, where he was introduced to the world of IT. Moving up the ladder to becoming a Data Engineer, Ahmad was able to understand and practice several IT concepts, including troubleshooting, networking, and systems management.

Today, Ahmad has hands-on experience building new ETLs from scratch, creating and deploying SSIS packages, SQL Agent jobs, and Microsoft Fabric Pipelines. He's worked on ingesting data from third-party vendors into medallion schemas using SSIS, REST APIs, Python, and DBT.
