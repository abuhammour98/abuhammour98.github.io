The journey from amps to analytics.

My path into data engineering started with electrons — a Bachelor's in Electrical Power Engineering. But as I pursued dual Master's degrees in IT and Computer Science, I fell in love with something else: the systems that make data move.

I cut my teeth in IT Help Desk, where I learned troubleshooting, networking, and systems management from the ground up. Today I combine that infrastructure intuition with modern data tooling to build resilient pipelines that teams actually trust.
